Submission Guidelines

We invite submissions of small academic articles to our page, focusing on political science academia, the political context in Bangladesh, and reviews of books centered on political science. This guideline will help you prepare your manuscript for submission.

Article Length

  • The article should be approximately 700-800 words.
  • This word count includes the main text, intext citations, and references.

Article Structure

  • The title should be concise and informative, reflecting the main theme of the article.
  • Provide a summary of the article's main points in 50-100 words.
  • Introduce the main topic and objectives of the article.
  • Provide a brief overview of the political context in Bangladesh if relevant.
  • Present the core arguments and analysis.
  • For book reviews, include a brief synopsis of the book and a critical evaluation of its contributions to political science.
  • Ensure that each paragraph focuses on a single idea and is clearly linked to the overall argument.
  • Summarize the main findings and their implications.
  • Suggest areas for further research if applicable.

Intext Citations

  • Use intext citations to acknowledge sources of information and ideas.
  • Follow the APA citation style. Example: (Author, Year).

References

  • Include a reference list at the end of the article, formatted according to APA guidelines.
  • Ensure that all intext citations are included in the reference list.

Example InText Citation and Reference:

  • Intext citation: (Smith, 2020)
  • Reference list entry: Smith, J. (2020). ‘The Political Landscape of Bangladesh’. New York, NY: Academic Press.

 

Formatting

  • Font: Times New Roman, 12.
  • Spacing: Doublespaced throughout the document, including the reference list.
  • Margins: 1inch margins on all sides.
  • Alignment: Leftaligned text.
  • Title Page: Include a separate title page with the article title, author's name, and affiliation.

Submission Process

  • Articles should be submitted via email to [politixplume@gmail.com].
  • Include the article as a Word document attachment.
  • In the email body, provide a brief introduction of yourself and a summary of the article.

Review Process

  • All submitted articles will undergo a peerreview process.
  • Authors will be notified of the decision within 23 weeks.
  • Articles may be accepted, accepted with revisions, or rejected.

 Caution Note (Originality and Authenticity):

  • All submitted articles must be original works of the author. Plagiarism, including copying and pasting from other articles, is strictly prohibited.
  • Authors must ensure that their work is not derived from any AI generated content. Articles created with the assistance of AI tools should not be submitted.
  • Proper citation of all sources and references is required. Failure to adhere to these guidelines may result in a rejection of the article and further actions as per our policy.
